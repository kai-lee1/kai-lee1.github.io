What does this do?


Long exposure photography is a photography technique that involves a long-duration shutter speed to sharply capture the stationary elements of images while blurring, smearing, or obscuring the moving elements. This kind of photography captures one element that conventional photography does not: an extended period of time.

Our site allows the users to have their video converted into a long-exposure-photography-like image. Without any complicated techniques and tools, this site creates an image of the similar effect. Even with a regular smartphone, the user will now be able to access the technique of long exposure photography
